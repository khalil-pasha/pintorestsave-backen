How to Download Pinterest Videos on Any Device

Pinterest is one of the most popular visual discovery engines, filled with inspiring videos, recipes, DIY projects, and more. However, Pinterest doesn't offer a built-in feature to download videos directly to your device. This guide will show you how to download Pinterest videos quickly and easily using pintorestsave.

Step-by-Step Guide for Mobile (iOS & Android)

Downloading Pinterest videos on your smartphone is incredibly straightforward:

  1. Open the Pinterest app on your phone.
  2. Find the video you want to download.
  3. Tap the "Share" icon and select "Copy Link".
  4. Open your mobile browser and go to pintorestsave.
  5. Paste the copied link into the input box and tap "Download".
  6. Wait a few seconds for the video to be processed, then click "Download Video HD".

Step-by-Step Guide for Desktop (PC & Mac)

If you prefer browsing Pinterest on a larger screen, the process is just as easy:

  1. Go to Pinterest.com and log into your account.
  2. Locate the video Pin you wish to save.
  3. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar.
  4. Visit pintorestsave in a new tab.
  5. Paste the URL into our downloader box and hit the Download button.
  6. Save the MP4 file to your desired folder.

Tips for the Best Experience

To ensure you always get the best results, make sure the video is publicly accessible. Private boards or secret pins cannot be accessed by our downloading servers. Also, be respectful of content creators' copyrights when sharing downloaded media.
